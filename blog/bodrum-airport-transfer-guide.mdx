# Bodrum Airport Transfer Guide

Bodrum-Milas Airport is the gateway for millions of domestic and international tourists visiting Bodrum each year. This comprehensive guide covers all transportation options from the airport to Bodrum center and surrounding resort areas.

## About Bodrum-Milas Airport

Bodrum-Milas Airport is located approximately 36 km from Bodrum city center. During peak summer months, this journey can take 45-60 minutes due to increased traffic.

<Info>
**Pro Tip:** Book your airport transfer in advance, especially during the busy summer season (June-September).
</Info>

## Transportation Options

### 1. Private Transfer Services

MOMTUR offers premium private transfer services with:

- **VIP Transfers**: Luxury vehicles including Mercedes Vito and Mercedes Sprinter
- **Standard Transfers**: Comfortable and economical vehicle options
- **Group Transfers**: Custom solutions for groups of 8-50 passengers

### 2. Airport Shuttle Services

Regular bus services operate between the airport and Bodrum center, but consider:
- Limited luggage space
- Multiple stops increasing journey time
- Fixed schedules that may not align with your flight
- Crowded conditions during peak season

### 3. Taxi Services

While taxis are available at the airport, be aware of:
- Variable pricing, especially during high season
- Potential meter issues
- Inconsistent vehicle quality and comfort
- Language barriers for international visitors

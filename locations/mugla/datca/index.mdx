## Your Routes to Paradise: Dalaman (DLM) & Bodrum (BJV) to Datça

Datça is accessible from two main airports, and Momtur Travel offers efficient transfers from both:

### Dalaman Airport (DLM) to Datça Transfers

Dalaman Airport (DLM) is generally the most common and convenient entry point for visitors heading to Datça.

*   **Distance & Journey Time:** The journey from Dalaman Airport to Datça is approximately 160-170 km, taking around 2.5 to 3.5 hours, depending on traffic and the exact location of your accommodation on the peninsula.
*   **Seamless Connection:** Our **Dalaman to Datça private transfer** service offers a direct and comfortable ride, allowing you to relax and enjoy the scenic route immediately after your flight.
*   **Ideal for:** Those seeking the most direct and generally quicker land route to Datça.

### Bodrum Milas Airport (BJV) to Datça Transfers

While further, Bodrum Milas Airport (BJV) is another viable option, often involving a scenic ferry crossing from Bodrum to Datça.

*   **Distance & Journey Time:** The land journey from Bodrum Airport to Bodrum city is approximately 35-40 km. From Bodrum, a ferry journey to Datça takes about 1.5 to 2 hours. Including the transfers to and from the ferry, the total journey can take 4-5 hours or more.
*   **Combined Travel Experience:** Our **Bodrum to Datça transfer** service can be customized to include transport to the ferry port in Bodrum and pick-up from Datça port, providing a unique and picturesque start to your vacation.
*   **Ideal for:** Travelers who might be visiting Bodrum before Datça, or prefer the experience of a ferry journey across the Aegean.

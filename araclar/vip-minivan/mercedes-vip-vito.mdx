### Premium Interior Features

Our VIP Vito vehicles are custom-fitted with executive interiors:

- **Maybach-Style Seating:** Reclining leather seats with ample legroom.
- **Climate Control:** Individual air conditioning for the passenger cabin.
- **Entertainment System:** TV/Monitor and premium sound system (Amplifier Area).
- **Executive Office:** Retractable tables and 220V/USB charging ports for mobile work.
- **Privacy Partition:** Absolute seclusion from the driver area for private conversations.
- **Mini-Bar:** Complimentary refreshments during your journey.

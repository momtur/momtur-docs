# Miya Beach Yalıkavak

Miya Beach is an exclusive beach club located in the upscale Yalıkavak area of Bodrum. This stylish destination combines pristine beach facilities with exceptional dining and entertainment, offering guests a perfect day-to-night experience on the Aegean coast.

## Beach Club Experience

### Beach & Facilities
- **Private Beach:** Pristine sandy beach with crystal-clear waters
- **Premium Loungers:** Comfortable sun beds and daybeds
- **Cabanas:** Luxury private cabanas for ultimate relaxation
- **Beach Service:** Attentive staff catering to all needs

### Pool & Wellness
- **Swimming Pool:** Elegant pool area with stunning views
- **Relaxation Zones:** Quiet areas for peaceful moments
- **Beach Activities:** Water sports and activities available
- **Wellness Services:** Massage and spa treatments

## Culinary Offerings

### Restaurant & Bar
Miya Beach presents a refined menu featuring:
- **Mediterranean Cuisine:** Fresh, locally-sourced ingredients
- **Seafood Specialties:** Daily catch prepared to perfection
- **International Dishes:** Global flavors with local touches
- **Beach Bites:** Light, refreshing options throughout the day

### Beverage Program
- **Signature Cocktails:** Creative mixology by expert bartenders
- **Premium Spirits:** Extensive selection of top brands
- **Wine Collection:** Curated list of local and international wines
- **Fresh Juices:** Healthy options and smoothies

## Atmosphere & Entertainment

### Day to Night
- **Daytime Vibe:** Relaxed beach club atmosphere

- **Evening Energy:** Transforms into vibrant dining and party venue
- **Music Program:** Resident DJs and live performances

- Regular beach parties

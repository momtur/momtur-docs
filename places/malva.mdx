# Malva Restaurant & Cocktail Bar

Malva is a Michelin Guide 2025 listed restaurant located at the luxurious Susona Bodrum, LXR Hotels & Resorts in Torba. This boho chic destination restaurant offers an exceptional fine dining experience that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of the Aegean region with a modern, sustainable approach.

## Culinary Philosophy

Under the leadership of Head Chef Gürkan Tümsek, Malva presents a unique gastronomic journey:

- **Slow Food Movement:** Experience leisurely dining with slow-cooked, gourmet cuisine
- **Local Sourcing:** Ingredients sourced from across the Bodrum Peninsula and Gulf of Gökova
- **Zero Waste Commitment:** Every ingredient is utilized to its fullest potential
- **Micro-Local Flavors:** Fresh Aegean delicacies with vegan options available

## Signature Experiences

### The Tasting Menu
The chef's 12-course tasting menu combines local gastronomic heritage with international techniques:
- Local prawns transformed into agnolotti with bisque sauce
- Red mullet served with Marseille-style seafood bouillabaisse
- Stuffed zucchini blossoms paired with mussels or scallops
- The extraordinary "Olive Oil Quartet" plate
- Specialties featuring Urla artichokes and Mount Ida cheeses

### Cocktail & Wine Program
- **Artisanal Cocktails:** Signature creations like "Spice Market" and lavender-infused "Artemis"
- **Wine Selection:** Curated collection of local and international wines
- **Sunset Drinks:** Enjoy spritz and cocktails with breathtaking coastal views

## Atmosphere & Experience

- **Setting:** Terrace dining with panoramic views of the Aegean Sea
- **Music:** Live performances featuring jazzy, soft funk, and soul interpretations
- **Ambiance:** Boho chic design from sunset to candlelight
- **Age Policy:** Adults only (16+) for an intimate dining experience

## Practical Information

- **Opening Hours:** Dinner service after 7:00 PM
- **Closed:** Mondays
- **Reservations:** Highly recommended
- **Dress Code:** Smart casual

## Location

Malva Restaurant is located at:
- **Address:** Torba, Kaynar Cad. No:15, 48400 Bodrum/Muğla
- **Venue:** Susona Bodrum, LXR Hotels & Resorts
- **Distance:** Approximately 8 km from Bodrum center

Experience the perfect blend of Aegean traditions, modern culinary techniques, and sustainable practices at Malva, where every meal becomes an unforgettable journey through the flavors of the Turkish Riviera.
